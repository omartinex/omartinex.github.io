A Simple Trick: Accessing Bank Data with Just a Number

It's surprising how some vulnerabilities are as easy as knowing a number. Let's dive into this straightforward trick in the XYZ Bank's mobile app.

The Trick Revealed

So, here's the low-hanging fruit in the world of hacking. If you know a user's national ID number, say "12345678," you can get their data by encoding it in Base64 with the prefix "01-". That's it! It's as simple as that.

Why This Matters

While the data might not be super sensitive, it's concerning how such a straightforward method can grant access. It's a wake-up call for institutions like XYZ Bank to tighten their security, even for seemingly minor details.

Low-Hanging Fruits in Security

This is a classic example of how some vulnerabilities are like low-hanging fruits. They're easy to spot and exploit, reminding us all to stay vigilant and not overlook even the simplest of issues.
